#### **Report A: Runner-up**

#### **Report B: Last Place**
Report B fails to meet the basic requirements of an audit report. It lacks **groundedness**, providing no quotes, locators, or specific data references to support its claims. While it correctly identifies the "mediator vs. confounder" issue as a material concern, the **coverage** is extremely poor, missing all numerical contradictions, table errors, and reporting gaps identified by the other two reports.
